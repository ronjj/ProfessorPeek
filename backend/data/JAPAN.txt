Code: JAPAN 1101
Title: Elementary Japanese I
Description: Gives a thorough grounding in all four language skills-speaking, listening, reading, and writing-at the beginning level. The laboratory provides explanation, analysis, and cultural background. Daily lectures are conducted entirely in Japanese.

Code: JAPAN 2201
Title: Intermediate Japanese I
Description: This course provides widely applicable language proficiency as an integrated Japanese course, which develops all four language skills (speaking, listening, reading, and writing) at the post-elementary level.

Code: JAPAN 3301
Title: High Intermediate Japanese I
Description: For students who have learned basic Japanese skills and would like to develop higher skills in reading, writing, speaking, and listening.

Code: JAPAN 4401
Title: Advanced Japanese I
Description: Develops reading, writing and oral communication skills at the advanced level.

Code: JAPAN 4421
Title: Special Topics
Description: This is a Japanese course to develop both oral and written communication skills focusing on a variety of current events and social phenomena in Japan.
